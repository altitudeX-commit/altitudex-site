How it works

From prescription to your doorstep in four simple steps.

1. Request

Patient places an order via the AltitudeX app or through a partner pharmacy’s portal.

2. Prepare

Pharmacy verifies the prescription, seals the parcel, and hands off to an AltitudeX operator.

3. Fly

Drone departs vertically, transitions to forward flight, and navigates via pre‑approved corridors.

4. Handoff

On landing, the app displays a one‑time code. Enter it to unlock and collect. Done.

The vehicle

A long‑range VTOL biplane glider concept with quad lift motors and a pusher for cruise.

Payload

Target 2 kg with modular payload bay options (standard, cold‑chain).

Propulsion

Four vertical motors for takeoff/landing; one forward motor for efficient cruise.

Airframe

Lightweight foam‑core with composite reinforcement for stiffness and durability.

Avionics

Redundant IMU, GNSS, health monitoring, and controlled transition logic.

Safety & compliance

Designed to align with UK CAA guidance for unmanned aircraft operations. We will only operate within granted permissions and approved test areas.

  • Pre‑flight self‑checks and mission simulation
  • Geofencing and dynamic no‑fly updates
  • Return‑to‑home & controlled descent modes
  • Observer‑assisted flights during trials; BVLOS only where permitted

Note: Final specs and procedures may evolve during testing.

FAQs

Where do you operate?

We’ll begin with limited pilot zones in partnership with local pharmacies, then expand as approvals allow.

Is it safe?

Safety leads every decision: geofencing, return‑to‑home, health monitoring, and cautious expansion of operations.

What about bad weather?

Operations pause in unsafe conditions. Patients will receive updates and rescheduling options.

How is privacy handled?

We collect only what’s necessary to deliver your order, encrypt data in transit and at rest, and give you control over your data.

Do you integrate with pharmacy systems?

Yes. Start manually, then integrate via API/webhooks for automated dispatch and status updates.

Can I help?

Absolutely. Join the pilot program or reach out if you’re an engineer, pharmacist, or community partner.
